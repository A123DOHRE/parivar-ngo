About Us

We Wanted to make a self Sustainable Surata Village Provide skillful self learning platform to the students

We provide great services and ideass

The Hunarshala is an unschool - self -designed learning (SDL) space,for students of govetrement senior secandry school surata[RAJASTHAN].IT is being supported by shikshantar (udaipur)and DS Group (surata). the learners are free to decide what they want to explore. There is no centralizedn curriculum ;rather, each learner is involved in designing their own personalized learning goals. We always start by asking them what they want to learn, how they want to learn, and from whom they wantto learn. There are computer Hub,Stitching Hub,Makers Space,photography,sport and other activity. hunarshala gives children an opportunity to first understand themselves,their uniqueness,their talant and their dreams. It is nurturing them to express themselves without any hesitation and then supporting them in pursuing their intersts and their leadership skills.People look at this as an alternative space,but this is not an alternayive space, it is the need of every child.It is what the mainstream should be.
